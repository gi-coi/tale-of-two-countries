A Tale of Two Countries

Dissecting Italy's healthcare gap

On this website, you will find some stories of healthcare inequality in Italy.

You will discover the invisible border shaping the lives of thousands of people in Italy, and why it exists.

You will learn about Giampietro's trips to Verona, Claudia's difficult choices at the hospital, and Sabina's battle to protect workers' health.

You will hear the voices of Luigi, Quirico, Gianpaolo and tens of others, and of their families who fight in their name.
